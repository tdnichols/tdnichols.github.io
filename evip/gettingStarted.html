Welcome to the eVIP Registration Process!

Before you begin, please review the Verizon Registration User Guide so that you are prepared for the information that you will need during this 5-10 minute process.

Registration for eVIP consists of 4 key steps:

STEP 1: Payment

You will be charged an annual user registration fee of $29.95 plus tax.

TIP: Please have your credit card information ready.

STEP 2: Identity Authentication

Your personal identity will be verified by Verizon Universal Identity Services before you can gain access to Oracle iSupplier, the system that enables electronic invoicing.

TIP: You must enter your full legal name, home address, DOB and last 4 digits of your SSN to verify your identity. This information will not be stored or distributed by Verizon or the NIH.

STEP 3: Setting up Login Information

You will set up a username, password and security questions. You will also designate your mobile device, phone and/or e-mail to receive one-time secure passwords.

TIP: One-time passcodes will be sent to you by Verizon each time you log in. It is also recommended you use your email address as your username.

STEP 4: Provisioning in iSupplier

After successful identity verification and account set-up with Verizon, you will receive an email from uis@betrusted.net and must select the “Complete iSupplier Account Setup” link.

TIP: Monitor your Spam and Junk folder in case messages from uis@betrusted.net are accidentally filtered there.

The Registration button is only activated by clicking on the link in your eVIP invitation email. If you are interested in participating in eVIP but have not received an invitation or cannot locate your invitation, please contact the eVIP Help Desk.
