Welcome to NIH’s Electronic Vendor Invoicing Program (eVIP)! eVIP provides NIH research and business partners (vendors) with access to real-time information on open orders and invoices. It also enables vendors to submit invoices electronically saving time, reducing errors, and increasing transparency. Go to About eVIP to learn more!
